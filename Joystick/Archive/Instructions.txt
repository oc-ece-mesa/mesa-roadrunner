CASE
Lower Radius: 	8.460 mm
Upper Radius: 	6.644 mm
Thickness:	3.000 mm

JOYSTICK
1. Make a sphere of size 17.50 mm cubed
2. Cut the sphere at 13mm. Only keep the top most portion. You will have made a dome at this point. 
3. Right-Click on the plate > Add Primitive > Cylinder. 9x9x9mm. Center the cylinder within the dome. Move the cylinder veritcally so that its face is resting on the x-y plane. 
4. Right-Click the Cylinder > Add Part > Sphere. Align the Sphere's x and y coordinates with the cylinder's. Move the Sphere up to 9mm on the z-axis. Play around with the scaling. Somwhere around 16x16x4 seems good to me. 
5. Add a torus to the last part that you made. Size 17x17x3.5. Play around and see what looks best to you. Center at 0,0,9 just like the last part you made. 
6. When you are happy with the result, Shift+Click+Drag, until you have selected all of the parts. Select the Mesh Boolean button. Select Union, Select all parts, and Execute. All parts should be as though they were one. 
7. Lastly, you will have to subtract out the joystick so that it fits perfectly...
